    Travelling and discovering new cultures 🌍

    I have been fortunate since my earliest age to have been able to visit different countries with my family such as the United States, Italy, Spain, the United Kingdom, Scotland, Tunisia, and Turkey. This fuelled my desire to discover new cultures and to develop my cultural openness. So, during my studies at IESEG, I chose to study for a year on the campus of the Universidad de La Salle Bajio in Leon, Mexico 🇲🇽, and a semester on the campus of the Università degli Studi di Bergamo, in Bergamo, Italy 🇮🇹. In addition, I chose to do a gap year internship in New York City 🇺🇸, between my Master 1, and my Master 2. These different international experiences enabled me to develop a real capacity of adaptation, and an open mind. I have always enjoyed integrating myself into a new culture and adapting to a city, or a new country. It is this desire to discover new things, and this taste for travel, that allowed me to learn Spanish and Italian, and improve my level of English. Meeting people and discovering new cultures made me grow and see the world differently. I am proud today to feel independent, and not afraid to step out of my comfort zone.

    Making videos 🎥

Creating a story through video fascinated me, and allowed me to develop my critical mind, my sense of detail, and my riguor.
